Work Experience

Summer Intern: MCL @ BU

I worked at the Multimedia Communications Lab at Boston University as an undergraduate summer intern. The lab focuses on Visible Light Communications. I worked on the network simulations using ns-3 module.




Research Assistant: MIT D'Arbeloff Lab

I worked as a undergraduate research assistant for a MIT PhD candidate. The research lab focused on building underwater drones for various purposes. In a team of four, we built an underwater drone from scratch. It was an interdisciplinary team. I got learn a lot of SolidWorks as well as simple machining. The drone was controlled with an Arduino and I was mostly in charge of the software as well as the controls of the drone.




Summer Intern: Cloudfactory

In the summer of 2014, I decided to go to Nepal and intern at Cloudfactory as a front end developer. Cloudfactory is a technology company headquartered in Kathmandu, Nepal. Their clients include various companies across USA, Canada and the UK. The company has an efficient and strong API-driven workforce for various purposes such as data and receipt transcription, image and video tagging, machine learning and many more services. I interned in the UI/UX team to create numerous webpages for the cloudworkers to do the various transcription jobs.




ECE IT Lab Assistant

Worked as an Lab IT Assistant at Boston University's Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. My responsibilities included maintaining the computers as well as other technologies in the instructional labs of the department. The department worked closely with the Engineering IT.




Bio Scientific International

Worked as an intern at Bio Scientific International, located in Shanghai, China. The company is a leading distributor in China for biotechnology devices from around the world. I worked in the services sector to help the current engineers calibrate and set up some of the instruments. I also editted their brochures, pampletes etc.




Dining Services BU

Worked as a food server at Boston University's Dining Hall for a semester. I always wanted to experience the food industry because I conside myself a big foodie.
